PERSONAL INFORMATION



NAME:   Mingjun Xiao (肖明军)   

Title:  Professor, Ph.D. Supervisor

E-mail:   xiaomj@ustc.edu.cn


He received double bachelor's degrees and the doctorate degree in computer science and technology from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 1999 and 2004, respectively. He was a postdoctoral fellow at the School of Information Science and Technology in USTC from 2004 to 2006, after which he became a lecturer in USTC. He was a visiting scholar at Temple University in USA from 2011 to 2012. Currently, he is a professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology and Suzhou Research Institute in USTC.

In recent years, he has led 9 national and provincial-or-ministerial-level research projects and participated in more than 10 national projects. He has published more than 90 papers on high-level international journals and conferences (such as IEEE Trans. on Computers、IEEE Trans. on Mobile Computing、IEEE/ACM Trans. on Networking、IEEE Trans. on Parallel and Distributed Systems、IEEE Trans. on Knowledge and Data Engineering、INFOCOM, etc.), including more than 20 CCF-A or Chinese Academy of Sciences top Journal papers. He received more than 1600 google scholar citations, among which 4 papers are cited more than 100 times. He has 2 authorized patents and published a monograph. He won the second prize of Anhui Province Natural Science (ranked 1st), the second prize of Ministry of Education Natural Science (ranked 7th), The USTC Alumni Foundation Young Teachers Career Award, CWSN 2009 and CWSN2012 Best Paper Awards.

He has served as the TPC, Track Chair or Workshop Co-Chair for many top conferences, such as INFOCOM, ICDCS, DASFAA, ICCCN, ICC, WISE, and is also on the reviewer board of many top journals, such as IEEE Trans. on Computers, IEEE Trans. on Mobile Computing, IEEE Trans. on Parallel and Distributed Systems, IEEE/ACM Trans. on Networking, IEEE Trans. on Knowledge and Data Engineering, IEEE Trans. on Service Computing, IEEE Trans. on Vehicle Technology, and so on.
