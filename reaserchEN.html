Lab Introduction

Our laboratory has been engaged in the research on mobile wireless network and information security for a long time. In recent years, it has focused on the research of mobile intelligent computing, mobile big data, blockchain and privacy protection. The relevant applications cover various mobile smart phone applications closely related to people's lives, such as smart city, intelligent transportation, intelligent life.

Research Directions

Mobile Smart Computing

Including edge network intelligence, federal learning, social networks, etc. Mainly use AI to optimize edge network resource allocation, use combination optimization theory to improve distributed learning and social network performance, etc.

Mobile Big Data

Including mobile intelligent group intelligence perception, space crowdsourcing, data transaction. Mainly use group intelligent collaboration to realize mobile big data collection and sharing applications, optimize the resource allocation of crowdsourcing applications, etc.

Blockchain and Privacy Protection

Including blockchain application technology, distributed system design based on blockchain and privacy protection mechanism. Mainly use blockchain and privacy protection technology to achieve decentralized intelligent collaboration mechanism
